Orchid (Rs.300/kg)

Orchid (Orchidaceae family) is a diverse and widespread family of flowering plants with blooms that are often colorful and fragrant. Orchids are prized for their unique and intricate flower structures, making them popular as ornamental plants.

  • Colors: wide range including white, pink, purple, yellow, and more
  • Light: bright, indirect light
  • Soil: well-draining orchid mix
  • Water: moderate; allow to dry out between waterings
  • Uses: ornamentals, floral arrangements, gifts

Tip: Remove spent flowers (deadhead) to encourage more blooms and reduce self-seeding.
