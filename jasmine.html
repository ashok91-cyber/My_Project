Jasmine (Rs.250/kg)

Jasmine (Jasminum spp.) is celebrated for its small, fragrant white or yellow flowers. It is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes and for its use in perfumes and teas.

  • Colors: white, yellow
  • Light: full sun to partial shade
  • Soil: well-draining, moderately fertile soil
  • Water: regular watering; keep soil moist but not waterlogged
  • Uses: ornamentals, perfumes, teas, traditional/medicinal uses

Tip: Remove spent flowers (deadhead) to encourage more blooms and reduce self-seeding.
