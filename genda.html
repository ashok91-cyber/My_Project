Genda (Rs.100/kg)

Genda, commonly called marigold (Tagetes spp.), features bright orange and yellow blooms. It is easy to grow, thrives in full sun, and is popular in gardens and cultural uses such as garlands and festivals.

  • Colors: yellow, orange, red, and bicolor varieties
  • Light: full sun
  • Soil: well-draining, moderately fertile
  • Water: moderate; avoid waterlogging
  • Uses: ornamentals, companion planting, traditional/medicinal uses

Tip: Remove spent flowers (deadhead) to encourage more blooms and reduce self-seeding.
