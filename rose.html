Rose (Rs.200/kg)

Rose (Rosa spp.) is renowned for its fragrant and beautiful flowers, available in a variety of colors including red, pink, white, and yellow. Roses are widely cultivated for ornamental purposes and are also used in perfumes and as cut flowers.

  • Colors: red, pink, white, yellow, and more
  • Light: full sun to partial shade
  • Soil: well-draining, fertile soil
  • Water: regular watering; keep soil moist but not waterlogged
  • Uses: ornamentals, perfumes, cut flowers, medicinal uses

Tip: Remove spent flowers (deadhead) to encourage more blooms and reduce self-seeding.
